Miga Korean Restaurant

Hot Stone BiBimBap

Their spicy pork stone pot (dolsot bibimbap) was surprisingly good! The rice actually burned a bit and I got a lot of crunchy bits. The pork was flavored well and I didn't have to add sauce. They also didn't add too many crazy vegetables. I would definitely get this dish again. In fact, I would give 5 stars alone on this dish --- Danielle C.

Spicy Tofu Soup

I always come here for their soon tofu, their side dishes are also good tasting,this is my go-to Korean restaurant ! ps, price is reasonable. -- Angie H.
